Effective Date / Fecha de entrada en vigor: April 5, 2026 // 5 de Abril de 2026

English

Sergio Montes Repiso ("we", "our", or "us") developed RogueVector as a free, ad-supported mobile game. This service is provided by Sergio Montes Repiso at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page explains how information may be collected, used, disclosed, and retained when you use RogueVector.

What RogueVector Collects Directly

RogueVector does not directly collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our own servers.

Your game progression, such as Banked XP, Run XP, unlocked Feats, current Loadout, level progress, and local settings, is stored locally on your device in the user:// folder used by the game engine.

Local Data Retention and Deletion

Local progression and settings data remain on your device until you remove them. You can delete local gameplay data by using the in-app FULL RESET option or by uninstalling the app and clearing app storage through your device settings.

Third-Party Services

RogueVector uses third-party services that may process information to operate the app, serve ads, prevent fraud, measure performance, and comply with legal obligations.

Third-party services used by the app include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob

Information Third Parties May Process

Depending on your device, region, and consent choices, Google and its partners may process information such as:

  • Advertising ID or other device identifiers
  • IP address and approximate location derived from it
  • Device model, operating system, language, and app version
  • Diagnostics, crash information, and technical log data
  • Ad request, impression, interaction, and performance data

We do not control how Google or other third-party providers process data within their own services. Their processing is governed by their own privacy policies and your privacy choices where applicable.

Ads, Consent, and Privacy Choices

RogueVector uses Google AdMob to display ads. In regions where privacy laws require it, the app may show a consent or privacy-options message provided by Google User Messaging Platform (UMP).

Where available, you can reopen these privacy choices from the in-app Privacy Options entry point. Your consent choices may affect whether ads are personalised, non-personalised, or limited under Google's systems and applicable law.

Log Data and Diagnostics

In case of an app error or service issue, third-party providers may collect diagnostic information such as your IP address, device name, operating system version, app configuration, timestamps, and other technical statistics required to operate and improve their services.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

RogueVector does not use browser cookies directly. However, third-party SDKs and services used in the app, including AdMob, may use cookies, software development kits, local storage, or similar technologies to provide ads, measure performance, and support fraud prevention.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data directly. However, data processed through third-party services such as Google AdMob or Google Play Services may be shared by those providers with their partners as described in their own policies, your consent choices, and applicable law.

Security

We value your trust and seek to use commercially reasonable means to protect the app and its data flows. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Links to Other Sites

This service may contain links to third-party sites, including through advertisements. If you click a third-party link, you will be directed to that site. We strongly recommend reviewing the privacy policies of those sites. We are not responsible for the content, privacy practices, or policies of third-party services.

Children's Privacy

RogueVector is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a third-party service connected to the app, please contact us and we will do our best to help direct the request appropriately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version on this page and update the effective date above.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at sergiomrdev@gmail.com.

Esta pagina explica como puede recopilarse, usarse, divulgarse y conservarse informacion cuando utilizas RogueVector.

Que recopila RogueVector directamente

RogueVector no recopila, almacena ni transmite directamente tus datos personales a servidores propios.

Tu progreso de juego, como la XP bancado, la XP de run, los Feats desbloqueados, el Loadout actual, el avance de niveles y los ajustes locales, se guarda solo en tu dispositivo dentro de la carpeta user:// utilizada por el motor del juego.

Conservacion y borrado de datos locales

Los datos locales de progreso y ajustes permanecen en tu dispositivo hasta que los eliminas. Puedes borrar los datos locales del juego usando la opcion interna FULL RESET o desinstalando la aplicacion y borrando su almacenamiento desde los ajustes del dispositivo.

Servicios de terceros

RogueVector utiliza servicios de terceros que pueden procesar informacion para operar la app, mostrar anuncios, prevenir fraude, medir rendimiento y cumplir obligaciones legales.

Los servicios de terceros utilizados por la app incluyen:

  • Google AdMob

Informacion que pueden procesar los terceros

  • Advertising ID u otros identificadores de dispositivo

  • Diagnosticos, informacion de fallos y datos tecnicos de registro

No controlamos como Google u otros proveedores externos procesan datos dentro de sus propios servicios. Ese tratamiento se rige por sus propias politicas de privacidad y por tus elecciones de privacidad cuando corresponda.

Tus elecciones de consentimiento pueden afectar a si los anuncios se muestran como personalizados, no personalizados o limitados dentro de los sistemas de Google y la normativa aplicable.
